ABOUT US

Kampala Caffeine started in 2020 with a love for great coffee and a mission to share Uganda's rich coffee culture with the world.

We source the finest beans directly from local famers committed to sustainable and fair trade practices. Wheather you're a coffee lover or just beginning, we offer quality beans and brewing tips to help you enjoy every cup Join us and exprience the true taste of Uganda coffee
